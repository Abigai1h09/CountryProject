Cuisine

Singaporean cuisine is a unique blend of Malay, Chinese, Indian, Indonesian, and Western influences, offering a wide range of flavors and ingredients.

Kaya Toast

Kaya toast is a breakfast dish that originated in Singapore and is made with toasted bread, kaya, and butter

Laksa

Laksa is a spicy, aromatic noodle soup that originated in Malaysia and is popular throughout Southeast Asia

Prawn Mee

Prawn Mee is a noodle dish that features a flavorful broth, prawns, and other ingredients
